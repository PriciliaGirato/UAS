I study at the Universitas Kristen Indonesia Maluku

Indonesian Christian University of Maluku (UKIM) is one of the private universities located in Ambon City. UKIM has various faculties that accommodate the interests and talents of students from various backgrounds, with the aim of producing superior, professional, and integrity graduates.

I chose to continue my studies at the Faculty of Computer Science, Informatics Study Program at UKIM because from the beginning I had a great interest in the world of technology, especially in the field of programming. The digital world is growing very rapidly, and I want to be part of a generation that not only uses technology, but also creates it.

UKIM provides me with the right platform to develop these skills, with relevant curriculum, competent lecturers, and a supportive learning environment. I believe that the decision to choose the Informatics Study Program is an important step in my journey towards the future I dream of.
